Learning to Love Again

Love can be a tsunami of emotions. When the tsunami calms back into the rhythmic ebb and flow, I find myself looking at the calm sky as I drift in the ocean. Being pulled here and there, I can feel the waves taking me on a journey. Where will I end up I wonder? I don't seem to care. A flower makes its way into my outstretched hand as I drift.
